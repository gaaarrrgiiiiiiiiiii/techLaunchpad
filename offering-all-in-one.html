Who This Is For

Three student profiles this program is built around. If you recognise yourself in any of them, the All-In-One Combo is the most efficient path forward.

Profile A
"I'm targeting a competitive domain and want every gap closed before season starts."

You've identified your domain, you know the companies you want to land, and you're not willing to leave any preparation gap open. The All-In-One Combo is designed for this exact intent.

Profile B

3 months is the ideal enrollment window. The program is designed to run concurrently with your coursework at 10–12 hours per week at peak - front-loaded by design so your CV output is ready before season.

At peak (weeks 3–7), expect 10–12 hours per week. The three components are designed to run concurrently, not consecutively - so you're not doing 36 weeks of prep. Bootcamp sessions are scheduled for evenings and weekends around your academic calendar.
3 months is the ideal window. It allows the Live Project to complete and be written up on your CV before campus season, and leaves 4–6 weeks for focused mock interview prep.
