Why It Matters

Technical Skills in MBA Placements - What Recruiters Actually Look For

Finance & Consulting
Excel is tested in pre-placement assessments - not just assumed

Multiple Finance and Consulting firms run Excel-based pre-placement tests. If you can't do a DCF in a spreadsheet or build a PivotTable under time pressure, you lose points in a round most students don't even prepare for.

Product & Analytics
SQL literacy is increasingly listed as a requirement - not a bonus

Product Management and Business Analytics roles increasingly screen for basic SQL proficiency. Not because PMs write production queries - but because being able to pull data independently changes what you can do on the job from day one.

Operations & Marketing
Power BI transforms how you present analysis in interviews and on the job

Operations roles involve dashboards. Marketing roles involve campaign KPI tracking. Walking into an interview knowing Power BI - and having a certificate to show it - is a concrete differentiator most MBA students haven't thought to address.
