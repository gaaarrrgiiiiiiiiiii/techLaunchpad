Bootcamp Structure

6 Weeks. Zero Filler.

Every module maps to a specific gap in your interview readiness — not a generic curriculum.

Week 1–2
Domain Foundations & CV Alignment

Domain knowledge deep-dive, CV review with your mentor, and a personalised prep roadmap tied to your placement calendar.

Week 3–4
Technical & Aptitude Rounds

Domain-specific technical questions, aptitude frameworks, and written/online assessment prep — the rounds that filter before the final interview.

Week 4–5
Group Discussion & Case Presentation

Live GD practice with cohort peers and mentor observation. Case presentation structure for domains that use it (Consulting, Strategy, Product).

Week 5–6
1:1 Mock Interviews

Two full mock interviews with your domain mentor. Structured feedback after each session. Mirrors the actual interview format your target recruiters use.

Week 6
HR Rounds & Offer Negotiation

Behavioural questions, "Why us?" frameworks, salary negotiation tactics, and common HR traps by domain. Often the round where prepared candidates lose offers.

Ongoing
Placement Season Support

Cohort WhatsApp group active through your placement season. Mentor availability for last-minute questions before each company's interview day.

Ideal Student Profile

Who This Is For

Profile A
"I freeze in interviews even when I know the answer."

Knowing the framework and being able to use it under pressure are two different things. 1:1 mock sessions with a mentor who placed at your target company simulate the real pressure — not a YouTube video.

Profile B
"I have a Live Project on my CV. I just need interview prep now."

If your CV output gap is already covered, the standalone Bootcamp is the right next step. You don't need the All-In-One Combo if you already have the project deliverable — this gets you interview-ready efficiently.

Profile C
"My college prep is generic — I need domain-specific prep, not a crash course."

Most college-run placement prep is generalised across all domains. If you've committed to Finance, Consulting, or Product, you need prep that mirrors exactly how those domains actually screen and interview.
